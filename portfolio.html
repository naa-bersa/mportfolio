About Me

I’m a UX/UI and Frontend Designer who cares deeply about how people experience technology. For me, design isn’t just about making things look good, it’s about understanding people, solving problems, and creating something that makes their day a little easier.

I’ve built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Figma, and Adobe Creative Suite, and I’m always learning new ways to improve my craft. I believe growth comes from curiosity, listening, and being open to new ideas, and I bring that mindset to every project I take on.

I work best in collaborative environments where everyone’s ideas are valued. I like to keep the process enjoyable while staying focused on delivering work that adds real value to the company and the people it serves. My goal is to create designs that not only meet expectations but help teams reach new possibilities.
